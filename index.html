The Rogue Assassin

The skills and tips for playing Akali in League of Legends

About Akali

Akali is a lone wolf, abandoning the Kinkou Order and her title of the First of Shadow. She stakes alone, ready to be a deadly weapon. She learned a lot from master Shen and has pledged to defend Ionia from its enemies.

Skills

Abilities

  • Passive - Assassin's Mark: Dealing spell damage to a champion creates a ring around the target. Crossing the ring empowers Akali's next attack.
  • Q - Five Point Strike: Akali throws five kunai, dealing damage and slowing enemies at maximum range.
  • W - Twilight Shroud: Akali creates a shroud, gaining stealth and movement speed.
  • E - Shuriken Flip: Akali flips backward and throws a shuriken, marking the first enemy hit. Recast to dash to the marked target.
  • R - Perfect Execution: Akali dashes through an enemy, dealing damage. Can be recast to execute a second dash.

Tips for Playing Akali

Tip Description
Engage Wisely Use your W - Twilight Shroud to reposition and avoid enemy abilities.
Combo Efficiently Utilize Akali's passive and abilities to maximize high damage output.
Map Awareness Always keep an eye on the minimap and be ready to assist your team.
